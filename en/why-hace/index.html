01
The founding choice

Oversized
by design.

The HACE turbine is sized at 1 MW rated while the system can theoretically produce 9 MW. This is not a constraint — it is a deliberate strategic decision.

Result: the turbine constantly runs at saturation, regardless of sea conditions. In 5 cm of swell or heavy weather. This ratio is what delivers a capacity factor of 50 to 90% — where offshore wind peaks at 30%.
