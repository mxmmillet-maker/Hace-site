Other wave energy converters stop or go into protection mode in bad weather. Every hour of downtime drops the capacity factor and makes the business model unsustainable.

The HACE solution

HACE produces in the worst conditions ‚Äî cyclones, rogue waves, tsunamis. The rougher the sea, the higher the output. The system is unsinkable and designed to last over 50 years.
